Welcome to Habits 365 Club

Building Positive Habits, Inspiring Bright Futures

    Shaping Brighter Futures Through Positive Habits

    At Habits 365 Club, we believe that positive habits are the foundation of a successful and disciplined life. Established on 14th November 2021, we are proud to be Goa's first habits foundation educational startup.

    Our mission is to nurture children with self-discipline, confidence, and a positive mindset through daily habits and holistic learning. We are an ISO 9001-certified organization, operating under the name Missal Edutech Pvt Ltd, with state-of-the-art learning centers located in Margao, Ponda, Panjim, Bicholim, and Mapusa.

    We are deeply grateful to the MBA students of Goa Institute of Management for recognizing our mission and publishing an article about Habits 365 Club, founded by Mr. Prashant Missal. Their appreciation underscores our commitment to nurturing positive habits and a growth mindset in children through personalized attention and holistic guidance.

    Why Choose Us

    Shaping Young Minds: Goa’s First Habits Foundation Educational Startup

    Habits 365 Club is an ISO 9001-certified educational center, registered under Missal Edutech Pvt Ltd, dedicated to nurturing positive habits and self-discipline in children. Founded on 14th November 2021, we are proud to be Goa's first habits foundation educational startup.

    At Habits 365, we believe that daily rituals shape a child’s mindset and future. Through our Habits Foundation Course, we have empowered over 7,000 students to cultivate positive habits, develop self-discipline, and embrace a growth mindset.

    Enhancing Literacy Skills

    Improved reading and writing skills for over 5,000 students through our online and offline reading improvement courses.

    Empowering Women

    Trained and employed 100+ Goan women as Phonics trainers, creating new career opportunities.

    Boosting Confidence

    Helped thousands of children gain confidence through public speaking, writing, and personality development programs.
