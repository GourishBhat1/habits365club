Phonics Teacher Training Program

Empowering Educators Across Goa with Interactive Phonics Learning

    📢 Phonics Teacher Training Workshop – Empowering Educators Across Goa! 🎓✨

    🚀 Habits 365 Club, in collaboration with Goa Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, successfully conducted an engaging Phonics Training Workshop at GVM, Ponda, where 100+ Primary teachers came together to enhance their teaching skills through interactive learning! A similar session was conducted at Marmugao Taluka attended by 200+ Teachers in 2024.

    📖 The session covered essential phonetic concepts, including:

    • ✅ Vowel sounds & blends 🗣
    • ✅ The magic of ‘E’ ✨
    • ✅ Soft & hard sounds 🔡
    • ✅ Engaging activity-based learning 🎲

    👩‍🏫 With a hands-on approach, educators discovered innovative ways to make phonics fun and effective for young learners! This initiative is a step towards enhancing literacy skills and building a strong foundation for children across Goa. 📚💡

    🔥 Impact So Far:

    • 🌟 500+ teachers trained across Salcete, Canacona, Mormugao, Tiswadi, and Ponda
    • 🌟 Creating a ripple effect in classrooms, shaping the future of education!

    Join the movement! Let’s create engaging, effective, and joyful classrooms together.
